Goals for this program:

Making a Snake game in JavaFX:
	- With a snake made out of rectangles.
	- Snake speed increases and snake gets longer after eating food. 
	- Food is spawning at random locations in the screen. 
	- If the snake goes outside of the screen or hits himself, the player is game over. 
	- Score for the current game and topscore are displayed at the top of the screen.
